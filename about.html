How We Vet Technicians

Not every AC company makes it into our network. We have strict requirements:

  • Arizona ROC License: Must be licensed by the Registrar of Contractors
  • Insurance: Liability and workers compensation required
  • Background Checks: All technicians are screened
  • Customer Reviews: Must maintain high ratings
  • Response Time: Commitment to fast callbacks
  • Transparent Pricing: No bait-and-switch tactics

Companies that receive complaints or fail to meet our standards are removed from the network immediately.
